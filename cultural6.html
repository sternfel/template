Anticipated Effects

By lowering the number of children exposed to inappropriate behavior and violence on the television, we can reduce the number of children who believe that these things are acceptable in society. Society depicts people who watch religious programming as more wholesome and kind hearted. Because of this stereotype, people who go against the religious way seem to be more rebellious. Even if you do not agree with the views of people on television, you can accept them for being different. Believing that all television induced violence will end if some programs are taken off air is highly unlikely but there will still be a decrease.
